28 April 2020

There has been an increasing number of observations in recent years

The videos of unidentified aerial phenomena that were recorded by Navy pilots in 2004 and 2015 and reported on by The New York Times in 2017, were released by the Pentagon this week, as verification of their veracity. The NYT quoted a Pentagon spokesperson who said that there has been an increasing number of observations in recent years. The pilots claimed to have seen UAP's by both radar and eyesight. Separately, public statements by Mr. John Podesta are continual insinuations that multiple governments have interesting material to declassify. Politicians with access seem to adopt the same teasing demeanor when asked about UAP's, as if the questioner is credulous. I'm grateful for the video and statements from the Pentagon. I wish they would tell us more.
